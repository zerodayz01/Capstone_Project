Student Retention/Completion Rates

  • College completion rates and same-school rates, especially those completed in under four years, are vital metrics in higher education. They measure the percentage of students successfully finishing their college programs within a specific timeframe, reflecting the efficiency and support systems of educational institutions. These rates offer insights into program effectiveness and accelerated pathways. They play a crucial role in evaluating student success and guiding improvements in academic programs and support initiatives.
