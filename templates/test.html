Standardized Exams

  • SAT and ACT scores serve as significant benchmarks in the college admissions process, providing institutions with standardized metrics to assess a student's academic readiness for higher education. These standardized tests aim to evaluate a student's proficiency in areas such as mathematics, reading, and writing, offering a standardized yardstick for colleges to compare applicants from diverse educational backgrounds.
